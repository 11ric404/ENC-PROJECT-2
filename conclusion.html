What You Have Learned

Here is a summary of the skills covered across all seven chapters:

  • Ch. 1 - Account Setup: Downloading Discord, Creating your account, verifying your email, and setting up your profile.
  • Ch. 2 - Interface Navigation: Discord's panels, locating the user controls bar, search, and the Home screen.
  • Ch. 3 - Joining Servers: Using an invite link, navigating the discovery page, and understanding a new server.
  • Ch. 4 - Text Channels: Sending, replying, reacting, editing, deleting, uploading, and using @mentions.
  • Ch. 5 - Direct Messages: Creating DMs and group DMs, using the DM inbox, and professional etiquette.
  • Ch. 6 - Voice Channels: Operating efficiently in voice channels, from joining one, to muting, deafening, volume control, screen sharing, and leaving.
  • Ch. 7 - Settings: Maximizing your experience through settings, including notifications, status modes, appearance, privacy, and safety.
